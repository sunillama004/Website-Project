~ Overall Webpage

For my overall webpage, I have chosen a dimly-lit background, images, and fonts to create a viewing experience that is less straining on the eyes, while still maintaining an attractive appearance. I placed my name logo in the top left corner, and all the navigation links are centered with a dim black color to improve the user experience. At the bottom of the page, I have added a short and cute footer with a top-left and top-right radius design that displays the copyright information.

~ Home page

On my home page, I have chosen a pen and copy background because it resembles a design background, and since I am currently doing designing work, I thought it would be a good fit. I placed this background image in the hero section to make a strong first impression. After scrolling down, I wanted to display a section dedicated to my hobbies. To the left of the hero section, I included an image of myself with a circular border, a greeting, and a brief description of who I am. As you continue scrolling down, you will see content that is dedicated to my hobbies. Each hobby has a short description and is displayed in a unique card format.

~ Portfolio page

On the portfolio page, I have chosen a library background as it represents a store of diverse books, similar to the versatility and diversity of my portfolio content. I have included a brief introduction about myself and my experience, along with an image of myself on the left-hand side of the hero section. As you continue scrolling down, you will find content that is dedicated to my recent projects. Each project has a short description and is displayed in a unique card format.

~ Contact page

On the Contact page, I have selected the 'Contact Us' background, which is relevant to the content. There is a contact form available for people who are interested in my profile can use to contact me. Additionally, I have added social media icons so people can follow me on various social media platforms.

~ Work Experience page

Towards the end of the Work Experience page, I have positioned the information for a better user interface, as it contains details about my experience and skills. Since there is a large amount of text, I decided not to choose a background image, and instead opted for a soft, dim, light blue color to enhance the user experience and make it easier to read.
